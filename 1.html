Hello Talk 이란무엇인가?

The very useful app "Hello Talk" to learn English with native speakers.
There are several good things in this app. Let's see what there are together.

The first thing

You can easily find a native speaker who use a language that you want to learn.
This app shows a lot of natives from different countires so you can also learn different cultures like music, food, or movive as well.

If you are intereted in a person who you want to be a friend, you can wave a hand emoticon to their and wait the reply to keep going on talk.

The second thing

You don't have to be worried about your levels because it suggests some tools interpreting the texts or voice automatically.
So you can use it when you don't understand what the partner says.
